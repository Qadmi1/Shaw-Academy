
  Q1:What is MAD?
	Mobile App Development (aka MAD), is both a Technical & Creative Profession. The art of communicating ideas and visual, 
        interactive content, in a very technical correct way.

  Q2:What is the MAD agenda?
	1-Design - Why, Who
	2-Develop- What, How, Monetize
	3-Distribute - Strategies, Publish

  Q3:What do you need to know as Mobile App Developer before development of an App?  
	You need to be thinking about the mobile app ecosystem and the world you're App is coming into creatio in and the context you're 
	app is coming into. It's no use running full tilt into development and not understanding the Mobile App world and the context of 
	that world.

//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

  Q4:Is Mobile App Development all about coding?
	No, unlike other software development it's also about having good understanding of the mobile app world and ecosystem so that you 
	can use every little bit of information at you're disposable to give you're App idea the best chance.

  Q5:Why make mobile Apps?
	Becuase we are spending more time on our mobile devices than ever before more than Labtops, Tv etc... and 90% of this time is
	spent in Apps.

/////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////

  Q6:What do do you need to do to make people spend a significant amount of time in you're App?
	You need to make you're App engaging and keep users coming back again and again and when you see people spend 90% of their time
	in Apps rather than the browser you need to start thinking why they do that and how can you design you're App to contribute to
	that 90% and you can take a portion of that.

  Q7:What is a Mobile, App, and Development?
	Mobile = Portable, Handheld. 
	App = A List of Instructions that Tell the Computer what to do next. 
	Development = The Process involved from Idea to Distribution.

  Q8:What is the importance of an App to to business?
	Most businesses report that an App would massively impact their business online.
	Here are some of the benefits for a business. 
	1. Build & Cultivate Customer Loyalty
	2. Provide Value to the Customer
	3. Engage with users where they are the most
	4. Create a Direct Marketing Channel
	5. Future Proof your Business
